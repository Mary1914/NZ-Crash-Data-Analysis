# NZ Crash Data Analysis (Official Statistics)

## Overview
This project analyses official New Zealand road crash data to identify
patterns related to crash timing, weather conditions, road categories,
and regional trends.

The analysis is presented using interactive dashboards built in
Power BI and Tableau to support data-driven road safety insights.

---

## Objectives
- Understand when road crashes and injuries most commonly occur
- Analyse the impact of weather on crash severity
- Identify high-risk road categories
- Explore long-term fatal crash trends across regions

---

## Dashboards Included

### Dashboard 1 – Injuries by Day & Weather
- Shows injury counts across days of the week
- Highlights that fine weather still accounts for most injuries
- Indicates weekday travel risk due to higher traffic volume

### Dashboard 2 – Crash Timing & Road Category
- Displays crash distribution by hour of day
- Reveals strong peaks during morning and evening commute times
- Shows higher crash counts on urban arterial and major roads

### Dashboard 3 – Fatal Crashes by Region (Trend)
- Long-term view of fatal crashes by region
- Highlights significant regional variation
- Useful for targeted safety planning and policy focus
