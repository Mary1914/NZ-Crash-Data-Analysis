NZ Crash Data Analysis

This project analyses official New Zealand road crash data to identify patterns related to time, weather, region, and road type. The goal is to support evidence-based road safety decisions.

  • Crash & injury trends by day, hour, and region
  • Impact of weather conditions on crash severity
  • Fatal crash patterns across road categories
View Dashboards ↓

Key Insights (Summary)

  • Weekdays show higher injury counts compared to weekends
  • Most crashes occur during daytime and evening peak hours
  • Fine weather still accounts for the majority of crashes, indicating volume risk
  • Urban arterial and major roads contribute heavily to total crash counts
  • Fatal crashes vary significantly by region and year
